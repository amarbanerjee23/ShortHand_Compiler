# GreenAI and C3-ECO-Aligned Evidence

ShortHand produces C3-ECO-aligned developer evidence only. It does not grant certification, does not claim zero carbon or carbon neutrality, and never subtracts offsets or avoided impact from the base footprint. External certification requires an external authority or auditor.


## AI backend evidence and claims control

Backend evidence records the requested backend preference, selected runtime backend, inference status, and fallback reason when inference is not executed. Missing optional SDKs do not fabricate energy, carbon, or inference success. Evidence reports remain developer evidence only and do not grant certification.
